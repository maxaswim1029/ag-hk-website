01 · About

A sourcing desk, not a showroom.

AG HK Limited is a Hong Kong-based vehicle sourcing company. We work directly with dealers, auction houses and export agents across Korea, Japan and China to find specific vehicles for our clients — rather than holding stock ourselves.

Every vehicle we recommend is inspected and verified before purchase: mileage, accident history, mechanical condition and export documentation are checked prior to shipment. We manage the full chain from purchase to delivery, including freight booking and customs clearance into Hong Kong.

03 · Process

From request to delivery.
